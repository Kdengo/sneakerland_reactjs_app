What is Arknights

Arknights is a Chinese mobile tower defense gacha video game. The core gameplay if that a tower defense game, with a number of characters ("operators") as towers.
The operators are divided into eight classes: vanguard, guard, defender, specialist, medic, caster, sniper and supporter.
